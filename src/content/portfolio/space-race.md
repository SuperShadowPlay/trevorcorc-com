[Space Race](https://github.com/codi-hacks/space-race) was a spaceship racing game written in the Lua game-creating framework [Love2D](https://love2d.org/). This was created along with the people at [CodiHacks](https://github.com/codi-hacks) - the former John Tyler Community College programming club. The game never reached content completion - but the minimum viable product that resulted was quite fun!
