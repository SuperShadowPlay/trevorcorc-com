Written in Vue.js 3, [Roll for XYZ](https://roll.forthe.quest/) is an online initiative tracker designed for use with DnD 5e. It aims to provide convienent and customizable features to help Dungeon Masters focus less on keeping track of who's turn it is or what health an enemy has, and focus more on having fun!
